Case Overview

Description

A robbery incident occurred at the Accra Mall on June 1, 2024, at approximately 14:30 GMT. Three masked individuals entered the jewelry store on the ground floor and threatened staff with weapons before escaping with an estimated amount of jewelry and cash. No injuries were reported during the incident.
